Fostering Excellence On and Off the Pitch

The Aba Bankers League aims to promote the benefits of playing football among bankers in Nigeria, fostering a sense of community, and showcasing the positive impact of sports on professional development.

Benefits for Bankers

👥

Improved Teamwork

Develop collaboration and communication skills that translate directly to the workplace.

😌

Stress Relief

A healthy outlet to unwind, reduce stress, and improve mental well-being after a demanding week.

🤝

Professional Networking

Build lasting relationships with peers from other banks in a relaxed, competitive environment.

Necessity for Nigerian Banks

⚖️

Healthy Work-Life Balance

Encourage employees to stay active, promoting a culture of wellness and productivity.

🏆

Enhanced Team Spirit

Boost morale and camaraderie within the bank, strengthening internal teams.

🌍

Corporate Social Responsibility

Showcase commitment to employee well-being and community engagement.

League Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must consist of employees from the registered bank.
  • A minimum of 11 and a maximum of 25 players per team.
  • All teams must adhere to the FIFA fair play rules.
  • Matches will be played on Saturdays at designated venues.
  • Team kits should be provided by the team itself and must be uniform and display the bank's identity.
  • The league management's decisions are final.
  • Violent conduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Hiring of external players is not allowed.
  • A registration fee of 100,000 will be paid by all participating Banks.
  • A cup/Trophy and Award will be given to the winner of the competition and will be fully endorsed by the participating Banks.
  • Every other match details will be communicated as at when due.
